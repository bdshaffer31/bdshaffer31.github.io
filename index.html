About the Game


Elemental Mastery

A tabletop skirmish wargame between small groups of powerful elemental benders.

Set in a world based on tradition and elemental mastery, command a patrol of 1-5 units ranging from sword and shield soldiers to powerful elemental masters. Dynamic, back-and-forth gameplay utilizing alternating activations and an action-reaction core keeps both players engaged as they attempt to outwit their opponents and seize victory.
